Gael Close | 2021- |

The website contains articles and book chapters discussing Systems Engineering methods applied to Microsystems development.

The focus is on lean methods for product development. The methods address all pillars of Systems Engineering:

  • Requirements

  • Architecture

  • Modeling and Analysis

  • Verification and validation

  • Industrialization

For each method, illustrative examples are given inspired from the author professional experience (15+ years in microelectronics).

Whenever possible, the actual computation tools used are open source to lower the adoption barrier. In most cases, the tools are parts of the Python ecosystem.
